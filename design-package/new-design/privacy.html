Privacy policy

What we collect

When you visit this site and analytics are enabled, we record:

  • Pageviews and the order you visit pages in
  • The referrer that sent you here, if any
  • Your country, derived from a truncated IP address
  • Device and browser type
  • Session duration

What we do not collect

  • No full IP addresses. The IP is truncated before storage.
  • No mouse movements, scroll heatmaps, or keystrokes.
  • No form inputs. The inquiry form content is only read when you submit it.
  • No fingerprinting. No cross-site tracking.
  • No data is sold, rented, or shared with advertising networks.

Your rights

Regardless of where you are, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any time, without giving a reason
  • Object to processing or ask for it to be restricted
  • Receive your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these, write to privacy@cloud-lord.com. We reply within 30 days.
